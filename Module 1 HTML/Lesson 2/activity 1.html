🌍 Did You Know? Welcome to the most amazing facts page on the internet. Get ready to have your mind blown! 🐙 Fact 1: The Amazing Octopus Octopuses have three hearts, nine brains, and blue blood. Two hearts pump blood to the gills, while the third pumps it to the rest of the body. How smart are octopuses? Octopuses can open jars, solve puzzles, and even recognise human faces. Scientists consider them one of the most intelligent invertebrates on Earth. ⚡ Fact 2: Lightning Strikes Lightning strikes the Earth about 100 times every single second. That is roughly 8 million lightning strikes per day! How hot is lightning? A single bolt of lightning is around 30,000 Kelvin — five times hotter than the surface of the Sun. Can lightning strike twice? Yes! Lightning absolutely can and does strike the same place more than once, especially tall structures like towers and trees. 🌊 Fact 3: The Deep Ocean More than 80% of the world's oceans remain unexplored. Scientists believe there are millions of species living in the deep sea that we have never discovered. How deep does the ocean go? The deepest point on Earth is the Mariana Trench, which reaches a depth of about 11 kilometres — deep enough to fit Mount Everest inside with room to spare! Fun comparison If you placed Mount Everest at the bottom of the Mariana Trench, its peak would still be more than 2 kilometres underwater. Source Facts sourced from National Geographic and NOAA Ocean Exploration.
